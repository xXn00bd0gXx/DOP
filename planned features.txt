## Medium version (~180 words)

**Depths of PowerShell** is an ambitious console-based RPG built entirely in pure PowerShell 5.1 — a single `.ps1` script, no installed modules, no compiled dependencies.

Pick from 8 classes (Knight, Mage, Brawler, Ranger, Cleric, Necromancer, Berserker, Warlock), each with distinct abilities and weapon affinities. Descend into procedurally-generated dungeons rendered as a first-person 3D viewport with a side minimap and fog of war. Fight turn-based combat against enemies with their own AI — they buff themselves, heal when low, and use abilities on cooldowns just like you do. Crack open chests in a real-time lockpicking minigame, manage gear durability, repair at the blacksmith, and juggle a weight-capped inventory.

Between runs, accept quests from the board (up to 5 active), train at the Training Grounds, hire companions, sleep at the inn, or risk it all for the Flying Dutchman's legendary blade.

Save anywhere with a Base64 save code. Daily Dungeons offer better drops. Hunt 30+ achievements. The full game runs in any PowerShell terminal that fits an 80×32 window.

## Long version / store-page style (~330 words)

**Depths of PowerShell — A First-Person Dungeon Crawler in Your Terminal**

Built entirely in PowerShell. No modules to install. No frameworks. No dependencies. Just one script and your terminal.

**Choose Your Path**
Eight distinct character classes, each with their own playstyle, ability trees, weapon affinity, and stat distribution. Will you be a Knight tanking hits behind heavy armor, a Necromancer draining souls, a Berserker trading defense for raw power, or a Warlock bound by a fiendish pact?

**Descend Into the Depths**
Procedurally-generated dungeons render as a first-person 3D viewport with a windowed minimap and fog of war. Move in real-time with WASD, fight enemies in turn-based combat, crack open chests with a timing-based lockpicking minigame, and survive deeper and deeper as the dungeons scale to your level.

**Combat With Bite**
Enemies have their own ability cooldowns, self-buffs, and self-heals. They throttle their ability use so combat feels strategic, not spammy. Bosses telegraph their biggest attacks — defend, or die. You'll juggle three combat stances (Aggressive, Balanced, Defensive), eight different throwables (including the 75-damage Bomb), per-ability cooldowns, weapon perks like Bleed/Burn/Poison, and a critical hit system that works both ways.

**Build a Hero**
Train at the Training Grounds, repair gear at the Blacksmith, accept quests from the board (up to 5 active), hire companions from the Guild Hall, and risk legendary loot at the Flying Dutchman's coin toss. Loot screens with arrow-key navigation, weight-capped inventory, durable equipment, and 30+ achievements give every run a goal.

**Daily Challenges**
A new dungeon every day with massively boosted special-item drop rates — Repair Kits, Extra Strong Health/Mana/Strength/Luck potions, and rarer gear.

**Save Anywhere**
Generate a Base64 save code from the Town Square. Paste it back next session. No save files, no cloud sync needed.

Requires PowerShell 5.1+ and an 80×32 terminal window.
